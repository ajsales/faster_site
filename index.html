Keynote Speaker

Sabrina Atienza | Cal c/o 2013 | Forbes 30 Under 30

Sabrina is excited about cutting-edge transformative technologies like Artificial Intelligence, Speech Recognition, and Recommendation Systems to solve massive real-world problems. She trained in Computer Science and Physics at UC Berkeley (2013). Prior to Qurious, Sabrina was a software engineer at Tealeaf (acquired by IBM for $50M) and software consultant for various tech startups. At Qurious, Sabrina raised millions in venture funding and built an amazing team that developed real-time conversational A.I. Now at Valued, Sabrina works with high-growth tech companies to build talented, diverse teams.

Her favorite reads are "The Power and the Glory" by Graham Greene and "The Way of Zen" by Alan Watts. In her spare time, she's probably binge watching Netflix, 90 Day Fiance, running, hiking, or reading.
